Early Life

Dr. Parashar was born on 5th April 1971 to a Bhumihar family in the district of Seraikela Kharsawan in the state of Jharkhand, then Bihar. His father Banke Bihari Singh was was the headmaster of Middle School at Chandil; his mother Indira Devi was a housewife.

In his school years, Sujay had good grades and was described as a bright and hardworking student who had a strong desire to learn. He spent hours on his studies

In Sujay's home there is 3 cows. Whenever he used to get free times Sujay used to take the cows to the field for grazing. Sometimes he also used to like to milking the cow. This used to be his one of the favourites time pass.

After completing his education at the State Substitute High School, Chandil, Dr. Parashar has graduated from Workers College Jamshedpur and Post Graduation from Co-Operative College Jamshedpur. After Post Gradution, Dr. Parashar join NIT Jamshedpur for M.Tech Program in Surface Science Engineering.

Dr. Parashar completed PHD Program from IIT Kharagpur. His research is on Nano Materials synthesis and Characterization.He is a member of five Six scientific committee. After PHD he went to DTU Germany for higher research. He worked at NIT Hamirpur for a year.
